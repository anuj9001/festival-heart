Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about donations, transparency, and our impact

We provide complete transparency through real-time tracking, photo/video updates, and detailed expense reports. Every donation is tracked from receipt to impact, with beneficiary stories and community feedback shared directly with donors. Our third-party audits and GuideStar Gold certification ensure accountability.

85% of every donation goes directly to festival support including food, decorations, gifts, and celebration materials. 10% covers program operations like coordination and logistics, while only 5% goes to administrative costs. This breakdown is verified by independent auditors and published in our annual transparency report.

We work closely with local community leaders, religious authorities, and cultural experts for each festival. Our team includes members from diverse backgrounds who understand traditional practices. We source authentic materials, follow proper customs, and ensure celebrations respect religious and cultural significance while bringing joy to families.

Absolutely! You can choose to support specific festivals like Diwali, Eid, Christmas, or Holi, and select particular regions or communities. Our platform allows targeted giving while also offering general fund options that help us respond to urgent needs and support smaller festivals that might not receive as much attention.

We offer flexible corporate partnership programs including employee giving campaigns, festival sponsorships, and CSR initiatives. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@festivalheart.org to discuss custom programs that align with your company's values and social impact goals. We provide detailed impact reporting for corporate stakeholders.

Yes, FestivalHeart is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, making all donations tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. You'll receive an immediate email receipt for your records, and annual giving statements are provided for tax preparation. Our tax ID number is included on all receipts.
